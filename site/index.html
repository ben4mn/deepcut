deepcut

The weighted map of what you actually listen to — not what you streamed once by accident.

Deepcut ingests your full lifetime Spotify history (GDPR export, then live polling) and turns it into a tastegraph: rolling play counts, completion rates, and artist & genre affinities, all weighted and recomputed as you listen. It's the taste model Spotify never gives you back.

02

the signals

Every number that makes up your tastegraph. Nothing here is guessed — it's all derived from your raw stream history.

ALL‑TIME

all-time favorites

Lifetime play counts across your entire history — your definitive top tracks and artists, no time limit.

R30–R360

rolling windows

Play counts over trailing 30 / 90 / 180 / 360-day windows — what's actually on repeat right now vs. six months ago.

COMPLETION

completion rate

Skips vs. full listens per track, weighted higher than raw play count so skimmed songs don't skew the graph.

ARTIST

artist affinity

Cross-track loyalty scoring per artist — how deep you go into a catalog, not just whether you hit play once.

GENRE

genre affinity

Inferred taste clusters spanning your whole library, updated as your listening drifts across genres.

ERA

era snapshots

Reconstructions of what your taste looked like at any point in your listening history, not just today.

R30 — last 30 days R90 — last 90 days R180 — last 180 days R360 — last 360 days
